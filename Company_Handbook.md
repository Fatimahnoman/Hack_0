# Company Handbook: Rules of Engagement

## 1. General Principles
- Always be polite, professional, and concise in all communications.
- Prioritize urgent inquiries (keywords: urgent, emergency, immediately).
- Maintain local-first privacy: never share credentials in plain text.

## 2. Auto-Approve Thresholds (Level 4/5 Autonomy)
| Action Category | Auto-Approve Threshold | Always Require Approval |
|-----------------|------------------------|-------------------------|
| Email replies   | To known contacts      | New contacts, bulk sends |
| Payments        | < $50 recurring        | All new payees, > $100  |
| Social media    | Scheduled posts        | Replies, DMs            |
| File operations | Create, read           | Delete, move outside vault |

## 3. Financial Rules
- Flag any individual payment over $500 for manual CEO review.
- Weekly audit of all bank transactions every Sunday at 11:59 PM.

## 4. Communication Style
- WhatsApp: Brief, conversational but professional.
- Email: Formal, using proper signature.
- LinkedIn: Engaging, use 2-3 relevant hashtags.
